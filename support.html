Support what we care about

XIVLauncher doesn't or won't accept direct financial contributions. However, we would be extremely grateful if you could consider donating to one of these, or an organization of your own choice instead:

  • Extinction Rebellion and Fridays for Future fight for governments worldwide to take action against the climate crisis
  • The Electronic Frontier Foundation fights for privacy rights and internet freedom in the US and globally
  • Chaos Computer Club e.V. fights for privacy rights and internet freedom in Germany and in the European Union
  • The Wikimedia Foundation fights for open knowledge all over the world
  • Your local no-kill animal shelter fights for your local to have a loving family

Direct support

Via stars

If you want to directly support us, we would be very happy if you could star our projects on GitHub. This is free, but helps our contributors regardless.

Donations

Individual developers often have their own donation links - these are typically linked within their plugins or on their GitHub profiles. Feel free to browse around - every one of them will appreciate your support. If you can't find a way to donate, the developer likely does not take direct donations.
